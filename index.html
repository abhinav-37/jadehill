Our Story

From a single warehouse in 1997 to a state-wide network today, Jade Hill Cold Storage has always been about one thing—protecting the harvest that feeds and sustains our communities.

We began as a family-run potato shed on the outskirts of Hazaribagh, serving a handful of local farmers who needed a reliable place to keep their produce fresh after market day. As demand grew, so did we. Over the past 25 years we have expanded into six modern cold-storage facilities across Jharkhand, each engineered for multi-commodity care.

Today our rooms cradle everything from potatoes and seasonal fruits to tribal-foraged Mahua flowers and handcrafted jaggery. What hasn't changed is the trust our growers place in us and the pride we take in delivering their produce to tables—and factories—across India and beyond.

Our Vision

To be India's most trusted guardian of perishable bounty, empowering farmers, processors and exporters through world-class cold-chain stewardship.
